CupFace

Created by Patrick Fulghum

Insired by Cuphead, which was developed by StudioMDHR.

  • Use the arrow keys or w, a, s, d to move.
  • Use the spacebar to fire weak but rapid shots.
  • Press the x key to fire bombs.
  • Press the z key to spin.
  • Use shift to shrink.
  • Defeat the Dice King before your health reaches 0.
  • You can parry red projectiles by spinning at the right time.
  • Shrinking is great way to dodge, but you lose offensive power.
